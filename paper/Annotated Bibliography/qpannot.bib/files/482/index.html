Recordings from the Estonian Folklore Archives 3

Anthology of Estonian Traditional Music Compiled by Herbert Tampere, Erna Tampere, Ottilie Kõiva

Eesti keeles

Anthology of Estonian Traditional Music provides an overview of the earlier folk music tradition of Estonia. The anthology includes the archival recordings 98 regilaul songs and 17 instrumental tunes from the years 1912–1966.
